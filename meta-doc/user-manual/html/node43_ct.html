Lists

Context-free syntax often requires the description of the repetition of a syntactic notion or of list structures (with or without separators) containing a syntactic notion. The repetition operator[ described in Section ]RepetitionOperator can be used for this purpose.

Lists may be used in both the left-hand side and right-hand side of a context-free function as well as in the right-hand side of a variable declaration[ (see Section )]Variables.
