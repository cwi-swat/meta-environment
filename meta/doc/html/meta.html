The Asf+Sdf Meta-Environment

Development Team

The Asf+Sdf Meta-Environment is developed and maintained by:

  • Mark van den Brand is responsible for the general design, compiler, parser generator, interpreter, and module database.
  • Tobias Kuipers is responsible for the text editor and the structure editor.
  • Leon Moonen is responsible for the user interface.
  • Pieter Olivier is responsible for the general design, interpreter, and debugger.
  • Jeroen Scheerder is responsible for the parser and parser generator.
If you have specific questions concerning the functionality of one of the listed components please address the responsible developer directly.
