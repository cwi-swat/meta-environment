Development Team

The Asf+Sdf Meta-Environment is developed and maintained by:

  • Mark van den Brand: general design, compiler, parser generator, interpreter, and module database.
  • Tobias Kuipers: general design, text- and structure editor.
  • Leon Moonen user interface.
  • Pieter Olivier general design, interpreter, and debugger.
  • Jeroen Scheerder general design, parser and parser generator.
If you have specific questions concerning the functionality of one of the listed components please address the responsible developer directly.
